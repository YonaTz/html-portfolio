The Best Anime according to Jonathan Vinton

My top 4 Anime

Soul Land

This has wonderful imagery - great storyline, worldbuilding, and characters. It's a Fantasy/Cultivation type.

Swallowed Star

This has incredibly vivid fight scenes. They can be long and drawn out - but very well made.

Throne of Seal

Gorgeous Scenery and fight scenes. Keeps you on your toes.

Martial Universe

Solid Anime - good fights, vivid images
